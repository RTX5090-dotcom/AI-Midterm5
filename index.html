Montefiore Square Park

Using AI to improve comfort and accessibility

About the Project

This project focuses on redesigning Montefiore Park using artificial intelligence. The goal is to improve comfort, accessibility, and usability for visitors.

Essential Question: How can AI improve public park design to better serve the community?

The Problem

Montefiore Park lacks enough comfortable seating and shaded areas. Visitors, especially elderly people and families, may struggle to find places to rest.

This creates an accessibility and comfort issue that limits how people use the park.

Site & Context

The park is used by a variety of people including students, families, and local residents. However, seating is limited and unevenly placed.

Some areas feel less comfortable due to lack of shade and rest areas, reducing overall usability of the space.

Before & After Comparison

Before

Limited seating and shade make the park less comfortable for visitors.

After (AI Redesign)

The redesign adds shaded seating areas and improves accessibility and comfort.

Design Options

Option 1: Add More Benches

Increase the number of seating areas across the park.

Option 2: Add Shade Structures

Install shaded seating areas to improve comfort in sunlight.

Option 3: AI-Based Layout

Use AI to determine the best placement for seating based on usage patterns.

Final Proposal

The final solution uses AI to place shaded seating in high-traffic areas. This improves accessibility, comfort, and overall user experience.

Reflection

AI helped generate design ideas and visualize improvements for the park.

However, human judgment was needed to choose the best solution and understand the real needs of the community.
