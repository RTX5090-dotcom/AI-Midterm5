Project Overview

Project Title:
Improving Accessibility in Montefiore Park Using AI

Goal:
The goal of this project is to improve accessibility and comfort in Montefiore Park by redesigning seating areas using artificial intelligence.

Essential Question:
How can artificial intelligence improve accessibility and comfort in public parks?

The Problem

Montefiore Park currently lacks sufficient seating and shaded areas. This creates a major accessibility issue for elderly visitors, families, and individuals who need frequent rest.

As a result, many areas of the park are underused, and visitors are unable to fully enjoy the space.

Site & Context

Montefiore Park serves a diverse community including students, families, and elderly residents. However, seating is limited and unevenly distributed.

This affects how people move through the park and reduces comfort, especially in areas without shade or rest stops.

Before & After Comparison

Before

Limited seating and lack of shaded areas.

After (AI Redesign)

Improved seating layout with shaded, accessible rest areas.

Design Options

Option 1: Add more benches throughout the park.

Option 2: Install shaded seating areas.

Option 3: Use AI to optimize seating placement.

Final Proposal

The final proposal uses AI to determine the most effective locations for seating and shaded areas based on how visitors use the park.

This solution improves accessibility, increases comfort, and allows more people to fully enjoy the park.

Reflection

AI helped generate design ideas and visualize improvements for the park.

Human judgment was necessary to choose the best solution and understand the needs of the community.

Project Information

This project explores how artificial intelligence can be used to improve accessibility and comfort in public parks in Harlem.
