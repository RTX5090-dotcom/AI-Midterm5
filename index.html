Montefiore Square Park

Using AI to improve comfort and accessibility

About the Project

This project redesigns Montefiore Park using AI to improve accessibility, comfort, and usability for visitors.

Essential Question: How can AI improve public parks?

The Problem

The park lacks seating and shaded areas, making it uncomfortable for visitors, especially elderly people and families.

Site & Context

The park is used by many people, but seating is limited and unevenly placed, reducing comfort and accessibility.

Before & After Comparison

Before

Limited seating and shade.

After

Improved seating and shaded areas.

Final Proposal

The solution uses AI to place shaded seating in high-traffic areas, improving comfort and accessibility.

Reflection

AI helped generate ideas and visualize improvements, but human judgment was needed to choose the best solution.
